About carnivalize.me

Carnivalize.me started as (and currently is) an avatar/selfie-masked-GIF generator.
It's an side-project/experiment handcrafted by Enrico (UI + UX + Front-End Code) and Andrea (DB + Back-End Code) and another bunch of people (Testing + Masks Illustrations) during March/April 2014. It's online —as a permanent work-in-progress— since May 2014.
It currently contains 21425 tags, 532 masks and 54322 pictures in 21 languages.

The project is inspired by Google Hangouts "Effects App", but differently from any Google product, carnivalize.me contains mostly anonimous uncensored user generated content. Masks are created using the simple mask editor and photos are taken with the in-app camera, and they are NOT affected by any kind of censorship. All of carnivalize.me content is rated using an age recommendation system "MPAA style" (like Gravatar) and age restricted content is shown depending on the age of the user (collected with a self certification form).

Technologies

A mashup of custom and OpenSource Scripts from various programmers around the world.
It's mainly based on HTML5 stuff, it heavily relies on auduno's headtrackr (for Face Tracking) and it's written in AngularJS (using linemnanjs) and Python (on GAE).
